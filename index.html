ABOUT ME

My full name is Laura Chapa González.
I am 24 years old, but when people ask me about my age I tell them I am much, much younger (just kidding!).
I have had a certain inclination for biology and math since I can remember. I used to dig for bugs and play with them, not cockroaches, though, those scare the bejesus out of me; I also liked to watch surgery videos and liked to know how the human body works.
Due to all these likings, I decided to major at Biomedical Engineering, as it had everything I expected concerning the anatomical and physiological part of the human being and engineering subjects like math, science, chemistry, physics, etc.
I like a lot watching football, but mostly when the Mexican National Football team is playing. I used to play this sport and some others like basketball and volleyball. I completely ADORE dancing as I used to dance jazz, hip hop and ballet. There was a time where I had to juggle between basketball, jazz and ballet all in the same day, so you can pretty much say, back in the day, I was quite an active girl.
